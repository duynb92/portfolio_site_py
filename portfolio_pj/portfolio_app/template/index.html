my story

Curious on how to increase productivity, speed up the development process, and working efficiently, I have spend time to getting familiar with Agile/Scrum theory & principles and being proficiency with many frameworks, from small-scale like Scrum, Kanban, ScrumBan.....to large-scale like Nexus, SAFe, LeSS....I have facilitated a group of Scrum Masters | Product Owners, training & coaching them on how to conduct Scrum in an effective manner. I'm also skillful with various user stories management, drafting techniques as a Product Owner. Certified Scrum Master & Certified Product Owner, are stand for my experiences.

Keep moving forward, Atlassian eco-system is my next interesting challenges. With hands-on experiences in project management overtime, it is a big plus for me. As a result, I'm currently a holder of 2 ACP program certifications, ACP-300 & ACP-600, this mean I'm qualified to become a Atlassian Technical Consultant. My next checkpoint is ACP-400, follow up with ACP-100.
